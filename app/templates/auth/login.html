SongRanker

How Does Song Ranker Work?

Song Ranker is a data-driven site made for music geeks! Choose your favorite artists and decide which of any two songs you like more. The ELO algorithm will determine a ranking for all the given songs you've chosen. Compare your results with other geeks, make playlists from your top picks, and consume data!

Why was this Created?

The idea of Song Ranker is that it's usually pretty difficult to rank all of a given artist's songs, even for huge fans. However, you CAN probably pick between any two randomly chosen songs from that artist. This is where ELO comes in. ELO was created as a rating system for calculating the relative skill levels of players or teams. This concept was applied to music rather than sports, and voila!
