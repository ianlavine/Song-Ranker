Song Ranker

Welcome to Song Ranker:

Song Ranker is a data driven site made for music geeks! Choose your favourite artists and decide which of any two songs you like more. The ELO algorithm will determine a ranking for all the given songs you've chosen. Compare your results with other geeks, make playlists from your top picks, and consume data!!

Why was this Created?

The idea of Song Ranker is that its usually pretty difficult to rank all of a given artists songs, even for huge fans. However, you CAN probably can pick between any two randomly chosen songs from that artist. This is where ELO comes in. ELO was created as a rating system for calculating the relative skill levels of players or teams. This concept was applied to music rather than sports, and voila!

Learn more about the ELO rating system here.
